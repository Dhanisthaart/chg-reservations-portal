Revenue Management Excellence

Room Revenue Standard Requirement

A. Revenue Culture

Revenue Management is a culture and philosophy

  • Revenue/Yield meeting should be organized once a week with General Manager, Director of Sales & Marketing, Director of Rooms, Front Office Manager, Reservations Manager, Director of Sales, Director of Catering and Director of F&B in attendance.
  • Develop culture of Revenue Management in your hotel: anyone should understand REVPAR, anyone should understand the reasons of saying yes or no to some business, everyone should sell not only price but also value, encourage upselling and promote cross selling.

B. Capture The Best Hotel Data

I never guess. It is a capital mistake to theorize before one has data. Insensibly one begins to twist facts to suit theories, instead of theories to suit facts.
-- Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, Author of Sherlock Holmes stories
  • Records statistics for knowledge based decisions. Train your team for recording properly reservation information: profiles (guest, company, agency), booking source, country of residence.
  • Keep consistent records of your data: reservations on the books, waitlist, denials, walk out, cancellations, rate codes, market segmentations, etc.
  • Automation – systems must be interface to maximise revenue optimisation, maximise efficiency and automatic data capture.
  • All report must be accurate and all statistical reports must match finance P&L report.

C. Pricing

Price is what you pay. Value is what you get.
-- Warren Buffett
  • Hotel to adopt Dynamic Pricing Models – maximise usage of dynamic pricing to include packages and corporate rates.
  • Develop pricing points as per expected scenario of demand: from low to high demand at your hotel versus pricing and availability of the competitors.
  • Encourage starting selling first lower rates and higher rates closer to the dates.
  • Rate Parity - The objective of rate parity in hotels is to encourage consumers to book direct. They should not find any advantage of booking via a third party website over making a reservation on our own hotel website. Think value added, fenced rates, consortia pricing, opaque pricing to go around this.
  • Rate Integrity – fair pricing, rationalizing pricing discount. Think Early Bird, Advance Purchase, Stay 3 Pay 2

D. Benchmarking and KPI

Know your enemy and know yourself; in a hundred battles, you will never be defeated.
-- Sun Tzu
  • Forecast accuracy – achieve 3% maximum (+/-) variance for the next month, variance between forecast and actual result.
  • Market Share report, based on STR - MPI/ARI/RGI result against the competitive set as stated in the HMA or otherwise determined by the management.
  • RevPAR and GOPPar result against budget and forecast.
  • Business intelligent report – subscribe to Business Intelligence solutions, make smart decisions by utilizing high quality, forward looking data.

E. System Automations

Modern technology has become a total phenomenon for civilization, the defining force of a new social order in which efficiency is no longer an option but a necessity imposed on all human activity.
-- Jacques Ellul
  • Effective revenue management is the key to maximizing profitability for any hotel business, ensuring rates are optimized as per the seasonal peaks and valleys for meaningful occupancy figures. System integration and automated revenue management systems is imperative.
  • Hotel's Property Management System (PMS) to have 2-way interface with booking engine and LHW if applicable.
  • Booking Engine to have direct connectivity with Online Travel Agencies, GDS, ODD and IDS
  • Revenue Management System is integrated with Hotels' PMS

F. Distributions

A product with better distribution will always win over a superior product with poor distribution or customer access. It's not fair. It's not right. But it's a reality!
— Stephen Davis
  • Optimized distribution mix of rates and inventory. Analyse each channel and understood what is the best mix at certain given time
  • Limit OTA to a maximum of 5 producing OTA for the market and must have direct connectivity
