Mission Statement

CHG Reservations Mission Statement

As the market leader in luxury hospitality, Capella's Reservations team is our guests' first host. We welcome with gracious, natural communication, personalize through attentive discovery, and confirm with immaculate accuracy. Empowered to anticipate needs and resolve challenges end to end, we craft every booking into a seamless pre arrival experience, converting every enquiry into a high value stay while upholding luxury revenue standards of rate integrity, optimal channel contribution, profitable mix stewardship, and purposeful upselling, reflecting our pride as Capella Ambassadors and our commitment to continuous improvement.

In practice

Market leader mindset

We set the benchmark for luxury reservations, expert, polished, and precise in every interaction and decision.

Guests' first host

Reservations is the first experience of the stay; our tone, speed, and helpfulness must already feel like Capella hospitality.

Gracious, natural communication

Warm, professional language with purpose; every written confirmation is clean, clear, and defect‑free.

Attentive discovery

Ask thoughtful questions, learn preferences, and tailor recommendations as local culturists, never one‑size‑fits‑all.

Immaculate accuracy

Files are defect‑free, names, dates, category, inclusions, payments, and preferences are verified before sending.

Ownership end to end

We anticipate issues and take responsibility through to resolution, looping in colleagues early when needed.

Seamless pre‑arrival

Handshakes with Operations are flawless so the guest's arrival feels effortless.

Quality revenue with integrity

We convert enquiries into high‑value stays while upholding luxury revenue standards: rate integrity, channel discipline, profitable mix/LOS, and purposeful upselling.

Capella Ambassadors

Our appearance, behavior, and communication (verbal and written) consistently reflect the brand; nothing is too much effort for our guests.

Continuous improvement

We learn from defects, fix root causes, and keep elevating our craft.
